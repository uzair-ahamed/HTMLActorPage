Vijay

Vijay is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil films.[1] The son of singer Shoba Chandrasekhar, Vijay debuted in the lead role in early 1990s, appearing in Naalaiya Theerpu, directed by his father S. A. Chandrasekhar.[2] He has sung for composers including but not limited to, Deva, Ilaiyaraaja, Yuvan Shankar Raja, Vidyasagar, Sirpy, Bharani, S. A. Rajkumar, Ramana Gogula, D. Imman, Devi Sri Prasad, Harris Jayaraj, G. V. Prakash Kumar, Anirudh Ravichander, Santhosh Narayanan, A. R. Rahman and Thaman S.[3][4] He is one of the most prolific singers among Tamil actors.[5]
